About    our mission

Tech it Out Philly teaches students how to use computer science in fun and meaningful applications. Together, we can build websites, make robots dance, program a light show, and build our wildest inventions. Tech it Out Philly provides a safe space for students to explore CS with talented mentors to help along the way.

FAQ

additional info

Is it free?

Yes, it is! Totally free for all. Thanks to Google's igniteCS program for funding Tech it Out Philly.

What do I bring?

Nothing but your excitement. We'll provide computers, hardware, food and drinks, and SEPTA tokens.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all. We will teach all of our projects from start to finish, without any assumptions about participant's prior skill levels.

Who's eligible to sign up?

Any Philly middle school (6th-8th grade) student is encouraged to sign up. We will cap participation at 40 students, so sign up early!
